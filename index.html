ABOUT ME

I am lucky to have a diverse family of artists, engineers, and designers. From a young age I was encouraged to learn for the sake of learning, love for the sake of loving and to expose myself to anything and everything I was interested in. I can’t remember a time that I didn’t crave problem-solving. Logic questions, math problems, puzzles - anything I could get my hands on. I was insatiable.

When I was first exposed to coding, I loved being able to work out the problems and pseudo-code the solutions. It seemed to me that learning a language was the next logical step, and once I started I was hooked. I take pride in implementing a craft well, and I’m passionate about polishing my technique.
